## How College Affects Spirituality

College is a milestone in everybody's transition to indepndence and adulthood. It is also pivotal in shaping the core values and beliefs that will stick with a person throughout their life. Among these are a person's religious and spiritual beliefs. A student may go to college with a certain mindset, but will almost certainly come out with a changed worldview.
